Training Health Baseline Survey for CBHFA Programs
Haiyan (Yolanda) Recovery

Philippine Red Cross' vision is to be the foremost humanitarian organization in the country. Its mission is to bring timely, effective and compassionate humanitarian assistance to the most vulnerable without consideration of nationality, race, creed, gender, social status or political belief.

This data was collected as part of a training exercise during a Community Based Health and First Aid (CBHFA) facilitator workshop and is for demonstration purposes only. Analysis is only for selected questions.

Data collected using formhub and ODK Collect.
